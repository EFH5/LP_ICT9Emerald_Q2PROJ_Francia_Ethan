The Tastiest Chocolate Chip Cookie You'll Ever Make!

Choco Chip Cookie Cake

Ingredients (for 1 cake)

  • ½ cup granulated sugar (100 g)
  • ¾ cup brown sugar (165 g), packed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (115 g), melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour (155 g)
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups of milk chocolate chunks (Or however much you want)
  • Dark chocolate chunks, or any other chocolate or topping of your preference

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine butter, sugar, salt, egg, and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Sift in flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture (Do not overmix)
  3. Add chocolate chunks. Optionally freeze dough for 30 minutes for more complex flavor
  4. Preheat oven at 180C or 350F. Line a baking pan with parchment paper
  5. Pour cookie dough into baking pan and top with chocolate chunks
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Poke the center with a toothpick to check if cooked, if the toothpick comes out with smooth and without dough, then its cooked.
  7. Remove cake from baking pan and let cool in a tray.
  8. Top with ice cream (vanilla works best) for even more joy!
