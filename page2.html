The Tastiest Chocolate Chip Cookie You'll Ever Make!

Chewy Choco Chip Cookie

Ingredients (for 12 cookies)

  • ½ cup granulated sugar (100 g)
  • ¾ cup brown sugar (165 g), packed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (115 g), melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour (155 g)
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 oz milk or sem-isweet chocolate chunks (110 g)
  • 4 oz dark chocolate chunk (110 g), or your preference

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine butter, sugar, salt, egg, and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Sift in flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture (Do not overmix)
  3. Add chocolate chunks. Optionally freeze dough for 30 minutes for more complex flavor
  4. Preheat oven at 180C or 350F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  5. Scoop and place dough on baking sheet with at least 4 inches of space from each other and 2 inches from the side of the pan.
  6. Bake for 10-13 minutes, until edges barely start browning.
  7. Cool cookies in a tray.
  8. Lastly, but defnitely not the least! Enjoy the cookies!
