The Tastiest Chocolate Chip Cookie You'll Ever Make!

Edible Choco Chip Cookie Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, heat treated
  • 3/4 cup cake flour, heat treated
  • 8 Tbsp. unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar, packed/li>
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 13 Tbsp. whole milk
  • 2 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • As much chocolate chunks you want (Dutch's is the preferred brand)
  • (Optional) Peanut butter, M&M's, anything you want!

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat treat the flours (DO NOT skip this step): Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Spread both flours onto a baking sheet lined with a silicone baking mat and bake for 7 minutes in the oven or until the flour reaches 160F then let it cool.
  2. In a medium sized mixing bowl, use a hand held mixer to cream together the butter, brown sugar and powdered sugar until creamy and smooth.
  3. Add in the milk, vanilla and salt and mix again.
  4. Gradually add in the heat treated flours until combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips until fully incorporated.
  6. Add more toppings if you want, and enjoy! (Refridgerate if you want to keep it, good for 1 week if so.)
